Campus Life

school library

Established in 1989

The School has a library with newspapers, magazines, grand collection of General knowledge, current affairs as well as reference books. Wifi Campus : To make the students updated with current affairs related to national and international subjects the school provides complete wifi campus.

School Laboratory

Established in 1990

There are multiple labs for subjects like- Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computers, Maths, Hindi and English Students are provided with all the gadgets, instruments and apparatus required for their efficient functioning.

School playground

Established in 1991

The school is well equipped with a playground which includes swings, rides, slides, cycles and other equipment for leisure. The school also has a basketball court, football field with a running track to encourage students to participate in sports and fitness.
